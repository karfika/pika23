The Sunset

Sunset always reminds me that no matter how hard the day has been, there is a beautiful ending waiting. The last light before night falls, as if whispering hope that tomorrow there will be new opportunities. There is a warmth behind the twilight light, which makes me feel comfortable, as if this world, with all its beauty, always holds precious little moments, waiting to be enjoyed.
